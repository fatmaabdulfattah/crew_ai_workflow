Coffee Machines in Egypt

Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee machine prices from various online retailers in Egypt. The study focuses on comparing prices, discounts, and product specifications to aid in procurement decisions.

Introduction

The purpose of this report is to analyze the current market prices of coffee machines in Egypt. The scope includes popular brands such as De'Longhi, Philips, Breville, Nespresso, Jura, Moccamaster, Gaggia, Saeco, Bosch, and Krups. The data was collected from two major online retailers: Jumia Egypt and Souq.com Egypt.

Methodology

Data was gathered by searching for each brand of coffee machine on Jumia Egypt and Souq.com Egypt. The search results were analyzed to extract product titles, prices, discounts, and specifications. The data was then compiled and compared to identify trends and make recommendations.

Analysis

The analysis of the data reveals several key trends:

  • De'Longhi and Philips offer the highest discounts, with De'Longhi providing up to 14.74% off and Philips up to 13.80% off.
  • Breville machines are generally more expensive but offer good value with a 11.11% discount.
  • Capacity varies across brands, with De'Longhi and Breville offering larger capacities (1.8L and 2.0L respectively) compared to Philips (1.5L).

Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are made:

  • For budget-conscious buyers, De'Longhi and Philips offer the best discounts and good brand reputation.
  • For those seeking higher capacity machines, Breville provides options with larger capacities.
  • Consider the specific needs and preferences of the end-users when making a final decision.

This report provides a detailed comparison of coffee machine prices in Egypt, highlighting the best deals and product specifications. The findings and recommendations should assist in making informed procurement decisions.
